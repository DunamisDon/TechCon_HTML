History

Historically, the conference has been organized with the backing of leading energy and utility companies, such as Pacific Gas and Electric (PG&E). It offers attendees opportunities to share technical papers, participate in workshops, and engage in networking with industry peers. A major focus of TechCon North America is to provide solutions to the challenges of grid modernization and reliability while addressing safety standards and regulatory compliance.

Mission

Each year, the conference addresses evolving technological advancements, making it a vital platform for discussing new maintenance technologies, predictive diagnostics, and asset management strategies within the utility sector

Past Speakers

The event has steadily grown in prominence, offering technical training, keynote presentations, and exhibitor showcases. It often draws professionals from across North America who are responsible for the ongoing maintenance and modernization of power grids.
