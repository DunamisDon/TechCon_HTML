TechCon North America 2024 will take place from February 14-16, 2024, in Sacramento, California. This event focuses on high-voltage electrical infrastructure and maintenance, featuring discussions, panels, and technical papers on asset management and innovations in the electrical grid. It's designed for industry professionals, particularly in utilities, electrical engineering, and asset management
